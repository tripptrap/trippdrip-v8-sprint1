About HyveWyre

HyveWyre is an AI-powered SMS marketing platform designed for real estate professionals, sales teams, and businesses who want to engage leads through intelligent, personalized text messaging.

What We Do

HyveWyre helps businesses:

  • Send personalized SMS campaigns to opted-in leads
  • Use AI to qualify leads and schedule appointments automatically
  • Manage conversations across multiple phone numbers
  • Track engagement and optimize messaging performance

Our Commitment to Compliance

HyveWyre is committed to compliant messaging practices:

  • All recipients must opt-in before receiving messages
  • We provide clear opt-out instructions (reply STOP) in every campaign
  • We maintain Do-Not-Call (DNC) lists
  • We comply with TCPA, CTIA, and carrier guidelines
  • We do not send spam or unsolicited messages

Use Cases

  • Real Estate: Follow up with property inquiries, schedule showings
  • Sales Teams: Nurture leads, book appointments
  • Service Businesses: Appointment reminders, customer updates

Sample Messages

"Hi [Name], thanks for your interest in the property at [Address]. Would you like to schedule a showing this week? Reply YES or call us at [Phone]."

"Hi [Name], this is [Agent] from [Company]. I saw you were looking at homes in [Area]. I'd love to help - are you available for a quick call? Reply STOP to opt out."
